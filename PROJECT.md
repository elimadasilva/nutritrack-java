# NutriTrack Project

## Vision

NutriTrack is a simple nutrition tracking application focused on helping users monitor their daily calorie and protein intake.

The main goal is to provide a fast and intuitive experience without unnecessary features.

---

## Target Audience

People who want a simple way to track calories and protein, especially Brazilian users.

---

## Main Principles

- Keep the application simple.
- Fast food registration.
- Clean and intuitive interface.
- No unnecessary features.
- Easy to maintain.
- Easy to expand in the future.

---

## MVP (Version 1)

The first version will allow users to:

- Add a food
- Inform the quantity
- Calculate calories
- Calculate protein
- Show daily totals
- Show remaining daily goal

---

## Food Measurement

Foods can be measured in two different ways.

### Unit

Examples:

- Egg
- Banana
- Apple
- Bread

### Weight (grams)

Examples:

- Rice
- Beans
- Beef
- Chicken
- Pasta

The application will automatically know which measurement type each food uses.
